Privacy & Data Disclosure

The Detroit People Mover station finder is designed to help riders find the nearest station and open directions in Google Maps.

Location access

If you choose Use My Location, your device may ask for permission to access your location. Your location is used only to identify a nearby Detroit People Mover station and open directions. The site does not continuously track your location.

How location is used

Location is requested only after you choose to use that feature. If you do not allow location access, you can still use the page by selecting a station manually.

Data we do not intentionally collect through this page

This page does not require you to create an account, sign in, or submit personal information. We do not intentionally collect or store your precise location through this tool.

Third-party services

When you open directions, you may be sent to Google Maps or another map service. Those services operate under their own terms and privacy policies, including the Google Privacy Policy.

Website hosting

This page is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log visitor IP addresses for security purposes as part of hosting the site. For more information, see the GitHub Privacy Statement.
